Medium-term growth · flexible horizon

Growth without a countdown clock.

SBR is no longer governed as a property deposit. It is a medium-term growth portfolio for a Singapore citizen earning SGD, able to tolerate market corrections because there is no mandatory sale date.

The 9–10% planning target requires substantial equity risk. If a firm spending date appears, the objective must change before the portfolio does.

Non-negotiable rules

No invented deadline

Do not de-risk because an arbitrary calendar date approaches. A documented use and date must exist first.

Simple beats clever

Do not add funds unless they improve diversification, liquidity or implementation after all costs.

Accept drawdowns

Equities may fall sharply. A correction is expected risk, not an instruction to abandon the portfolio.

Match currency at exit

Once a future SGD use is credible, progressively move the required amount into SGD-safe assets.

The runway, not the road

The live SBR app should show portfolio runway: current mode, allocation health, drawdown resilience and whether an exit window exists. Property, CPF and home-readiness language should disappear.
