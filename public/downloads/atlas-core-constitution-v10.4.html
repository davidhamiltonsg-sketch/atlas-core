Article III · Contributions

Contribution-first, whole-share DCA

  1. Add the contribution and carried cash to this portfolio’s DCA bank.
  2. Reserve estimated FX and commission.
  3. Exclude breached, paused or stale-data-dependent sleeves.
  4. Route money to the furthest-underweight eligible sleeve.
  5. Buy whole units and reconcile to confirmed IBKR executions.
  6. Carry every unused cent. Never pretend banked cash was invested.

Worked example

If S$1,080 is available and a unit costs S$310 after FX and commission, buy three for S$930 and carry S$150. A partial fill debits confirmed cost only; retries cannot duplicate it.

Article V · Behaviour and liquidity

Survive the plan

  • Green: income secure and external SGD reserve funded—continue.
  • Amber: income uncertain or reserve used—pause optional increases and rebuild liquidity.
  • Red: income lost or reserve inadequate—suspend contributions.

No leverage, margin, prediction-led changes or panic sales. Wait 72 hours before an unscheduled discretionary sale unless delay creates legal or operational danger. Personality labels are not investment rules; observable liquidity and behaviour are.

Article VI · Governance

Reviews and amendments

  • Monthly: reconcile IBKR holdings, cash, executions, dividends, fees, FX and adjustments.
  • Quarterly: review bands, look-through, freshness, fund status and contribution capacity.
  • Annually: review objective, external liquidity, tax/legal circumstances, beneficiaries and vehicles.
  • Amendment: written evidence, version increment and 30-day cooling-off except genuine emergencies.

Archive old constitutions as history, but never allow them to remain active runtime instructions.
