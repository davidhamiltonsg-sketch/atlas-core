The 10% figure is an objective used for planning. It is not an expected annual result and is not guaranteed. A severe temporary drawdown remains plausible.

Non-negotiable rules

Rebalance with cash first

Direct new contributions to the most underweight eligible holding before considering a sale.

Cap overlapping bets

EQQQ plus SEMI may not exceed 25%. EQQQ, SEMI and Bitcoin together may not exceed 33%.

No crash selling

A falling market is not evidence that the constitution failed. Selling requires a rule breach or changed life need.

Review rarely

Check contributions monthly, bands quarterly and the constitution annually. Headlines trigger no review.

The dashboard’s first question

“What does the constitution permit me to do today?” The live app should answer that before it shows performance. The constitution remains available as a quiet, printable document when no market information is wanted.
